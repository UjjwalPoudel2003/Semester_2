#VI commands
$vi filename.txt (to create the file)
esc + L (to move right)
esc + h (to move left)
esc + k (move top)
esc + j (move bottom)
esc + : + w + q (save and quit)
esc + i (to insert)
esc + <no of rows of lines from vursor position> + yy (to yank - copy)
esc + p (to yank - paste)
esc + a (to append)
esc + x (single letter)
esc + dd (line deletion)
